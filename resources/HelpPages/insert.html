Inserting text while in command mode.

Pressing the keys will first make the text area editable and then you can use text area normally.

  • You can press 'i' to go into insertion mode and insert after the cursor.
  • You can press 'I' to go into insertion mode and insert at the beginning of the line.
  • You can press 'a' to go into insertion mode and insert at the position left to the current curson position.
  • You can press 'A' to go into insertion mode and insert at the end of the current line.
